FHS Math Team

This is the new website for the FHS Math Team. We participate in a number of math contests both at FHS and local colleges throughout the school year. We welcome any level of student who likes math and encourage everyone to engage in mathematical problem solving.

The only joining requirements are

  • 6 service hours
  • $10 membership fees
  • 50% monthly meeting attendance, and/or competition attendance

We generally meet in room H222.
