This is the website for Fishers High Schools' Math Team. We participate in a number of math contests both at FHS and local colleges throughout the school year. We welcome any level of student who likes math and encourage everyone to engage in mathematical problem solving.

The only joining requirements are

  • 6 service hours
  • $10 year-long membership fee
  • Participation in at least half of the events. (meetings, competitions, and IMLs)

Most of our meetings are held in room H222.

If you have any questions, you can email our teacher sponsors.
